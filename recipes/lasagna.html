Easy Homemade Lasagna

All you’ll need for this easy lasagna recipe is one pan, one bowl, and a 9×13 baking dish! A quick overview of the layers:

  • Cheese Filling: Mix the ricotta and cheeses (per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl, and set aside. No ricotta? No problem, cottage cheese works just fine in this recipe!
  • Meat Sauce: Meat Sauce Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes.
  • Layer Together: Layer Together Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling
